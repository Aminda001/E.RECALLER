Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button on the homepage or login page. Fill in your details including name, password, and mobile number to create your account.

How do I log in to my account?

Go to the login page and enter your username and password. Click the "Log In" button to access your account.

Account Management

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your username and mobile number to verify your identity. You'll then be able to set a new password.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your Profile page after logging in. You can edit your information and click "Save Changes" to update your details.
